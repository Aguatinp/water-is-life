Water Is Life For Craighouse

Craighouse School and Round Square: A Joint Project with WATERisLIFE

Craighouse School, together with the international network Round Square, is launching a meaningful project in collaboration with WATERisLIFE. The goal of this initiative is to help Chilean communities that do not have access to clean drinking water.

As part of the project, each grade from First Grade (Primero Básico) to Twelfth Grade (Cuarto Medio) will participate by donating a Bucket Ultra Filter – a highly effective product developed by WATERisLIFE that provides safe and clean water. Every class, across all generations, will contribute to this cause, showing the power of unity and social responsibility within our school.

This project is not only about giving, but also about learning and raising awareness. It reflects the values of Round Square and Craighouse: service, compassion, and global citizenship.

Together, we can make a real difference for fellow Chileans in need.
